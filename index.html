Hi, I'm Rishita

I build things at the intersection of design, data, and curiosity. From fashion-tech side projects to marketing dashboards, I love solving messy problems with clean thinking. But beyond the resume, I care about why we create and who it helps.

This space? Just my way of bringing that journey to life.
(And yes, that’s my AI doppelgänger. She’s quieter than I am, but a close-enough hello until I can greet you in person!!)

Know Me More

Research

My most recent paper explores how AI can bridge the gap between art and fashion by using GAN and Pix2Pix models to generate garment designs from abstract sketches. I presented this work at the International Conference on Applications of AI and ML (ICAML), where it opened up conversations about how creativity and computation can coexist in the design process.

Currently Learning

At Duke University, I’m learning how to work with data in ways that actually say something. I’m also trying to understand how economies behave and how tools like Python and R can help make sense of it all.

Lately on My Mind

Fashion-tech keeps pulling me in. There’s something about blending data, design, and AI that makes expression feel more alive, from fabrics that respond to the body to algorithms that understand personal style.
